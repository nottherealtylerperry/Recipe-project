Lemon Garlic Salmon

Description

Fresh salmon fillets cooked with garlic, butter, and lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Season salmon fillets on both sides with lemon pepper
  2. In large skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat
  3. Stir in garlic; add salmon and cook for 10 minutes per inch of thickness
  4. Sprinkle with lemon juice before serving
