Chicken Katsu

Description

Japanese style fried chicken served over rice

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, pounded to 1/2 inch thickness
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 cup oil for frying
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season shicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper
  2. Place flour, egg, and panko crumbs in separate shallow dishes
  3. Coat the chicken breasts in flour, shaking of excess
  4. Dip coated breats into egg then press into bread crumbs until well coated on both sides
  5. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in large skilled over medium-high heat
  6. Place chicken in hot oil and cook for 3 to 4 minutes on each side until golden brown
